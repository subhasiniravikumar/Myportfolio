- **What it does:** Elements fade in, slide, and zoom as you scroll down
- **Effects included:**

  - Left/right slide effects on achievements
  - Zoom-in on stat cards
- **Performance:** Optimized to trigger once for smooth experience
- **Benefit:** Makes your portfolio feel modern and interactive

## 🚀 How to Use Each Feature

### Dark Mode
1. Click the **moon icon** (🌙) in the top navigation bar
2. Theme switches to dark mode with pink accents
3. Preference is saved - it will remember on next visit

### Stats Counters
1. Scroll down to the section below the hero
2. Watch the numbers animate counting up
3. Each stat card has a hover lift effect

### Scroll Animations
1. As you scroll down the page, elements animate in
2. Try scrolling through different sections
3. Each section has unique entry animations

### Typed Hero
1. Look at the hero section (title area at top)
2. Watch the rotating text types and backtracks
3. It continuously cycles through 5 different roles

### Project Cards
1. Scroll to the Projects section
2. Hover over any project card to see:
   - Lift effect
   - Tech badge highlights
   - "Visit Site" link appears
3. Click "Visit Site" to see the live project

### Skills Chart
1. Scroll to Skills section
2. You'll see a colorful radar chart at the top
3. Hover over data points to see exact proficiency %
4. Below are traditional progress bars for comparison

### FAB Menu
1. Look for the floating pink button in bottom-right corner
2. Click it to expand quick action buttons
3. Use to quickly contact or scroll to top
4. Button hides when at top of page

### Resume Download
1. Scroll to Contact section
2. Click **"Download Resume"** button
3. PDF automatically downloads to your Downloads folder
4. Open to view professional resume
